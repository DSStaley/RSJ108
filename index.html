Darrell Staley's Preparing for a Great Interview

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

Do you want to interview a movie star, musician, or professional athlete? Being prepared will set the stage for a great interview! Here are the important steps to be prepared for a successful interview.

Assembly time: 120 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

  • Things You'll Need

  • Research Material
  • Laptop
  • Sample Questions
  • Film Crew
  • Comfortable Setting
  • Water

  1. Do Your Research

    Take the time to learn about the person you are interviewing. It is very important to be familiar with the person and his or her contributions in their field.

  2. Know Your Objective

    Be sure to answer the following questions when preparing for the interview: Why you are interviewing this person? Did he or she do something recently that will be interesting to the audience? Why is this person newsworthy?

  3. Prepare Questions

    Always prepare your interview questions in advance and be sure the questions are relevant to the purpose of the interview.

  4. Prepare the Setting

    Make sure the setting is comfortable.Is it formal? Informal? Pay attention to seating, lighting, noise, and distractions. Always have water available.

  • Be ready!

    Is your camera crew and other staff prepared? Are you are dressed and groomed appropriately? Are you sure your interviewee will be comfortable and at ease? Be calm and confident; it matters.

  • Practice

    Practice conducting the interview with a colleague or friend.
